CSS3 Animations

Easy to define, easy to use!
CSS3 Animations work out of the box with:

  • ng-repeat
  • ng-view
  • ng-include
  • ng-switch
  • ng-if
  • ng-class
  • ng-show, ng-hide
  • forms
Anything where angular gives you classes!
