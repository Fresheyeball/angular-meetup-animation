Animations!

Since angular 1.2.x animations are class based regardless of if the animation is css3 or javascript based. Out of the box angular will add and remove classes for:

  • ng-repeat
  • ng-view
  • ng-include
  • ng-switch
  • ng-if
  • ng-class
  • ng-show, ng-hide
  • forms

When animating you should be considering:

  • Why am I animating this element?
  • Visual Context
  • Relationships to other animations
  • Feeling of Mass
  • Feeling of Speed
  • Feeling of Power
